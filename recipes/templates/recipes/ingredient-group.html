What is an Ingredient Group?

An Ingredient Group is a way to have different variations of a recipe. Example, one variation that has gluten and another that is gluten-free.

They also allow the ability to organize the ingredients of a recipe into groups to help identify which ingredients are for the main dish versus the ones used to make the dressing, frosting, etc.
